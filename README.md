Census is a survey conducted in the UK every ten years to obtain information of the population, 
in this case we have been given a fictional census of a town between two major cities. The local 
government must decide the utilization of an open plot of land which is vacant and the other 
major investments should be planned because of population demographic and economic 
requirement(Ilyas and Rekatsinas, 2022). The information offered covers house numbers, street 
names, occupants’ details, other information about marital status, occupation, diseases and even 
religion. 
This report provides analysis to determine: 
1. As for the plot of land it is still vacant, what should be constructed on the vacant piece 
of land. 
2. To what extent are infrastructure and social services that are more important for 
investment?
